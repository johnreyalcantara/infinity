# Infinity B2B Pricing Strategy Guide (Philippines Market)

This document outlines the recommended pricing model for acquiring local businesses, retail stores, and franchises. Given that the Infinity system physically provides hardware and installation labor, the pricing model utilizes a two-tiered path.

---

## 1. Setup & Hardware Cost (One-Time Fee)
**Recommended Price:** `₱7,500 to ₱9,500`

### Purpose
To completely protect the startup from bleeding cash on physical equipment. B2B software is highly profitable because code scales for free, but hardware does not. 

### Hard Cost Estimates per Tenant
- Bluetooth Wireless Barcode Scanner: `~₱1,200`
- IP Security Camera (e.g., HD Tapo/Xiaomi): `~₱1,800`
- Transpo & Labor Setup Time (1-Day Deployment): `~₱1,500`
- **Total Physical Cost:** `~₱4,500`

By charging `₱7,500+`, you instantly cover real-world expenses and generate immediate cash flow. This is a very easy sell; traditional bulky POS registers cost small business owners upwards of `₱20,000` to `₱40,000`.

---

## 2. Platform Subscription (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
**Recommended Price:** `₱1,499 to ₱1,999 / month` per branch.

### Purpose
To cover cloud hosting, syncing data, continuous development, and generate passive profit.

### Value Proposition Justification
- Direct competitors (Utak POS, StoreHub) charge around `₱1,500/month` purely for terminal software.
- Infinity provides **Offline Resiliency** AND remote **Live CCTV Storefront Feeds**. This places the platform in a premium operational security tier.
- Pitch logic downsized over time: *“₱1,499 a month is only ₱50 a day. Less than the cost of a single cup of coffee, you secure your entire inventory and store against internet dropping and physical theft.”*

---

## 3. Go-to-Market Promotion Strategy
**"The Annual Lock-in Promo"**

To acquire your first 50-100 customers incredibly fast and establish a reputation, remove the upfront friction:
> *"Pay 1 Year of Software Access in advance (₱1,499 x 12 = ₱17,988), and we will entirely waive the ₱7,500 setup fee! The hardware is yours for free."*

**Why this works:**
1. Small business owners hate large "pointless" fees but like getting physical things for free.
2. It secures you `₱17,988` in cash *immediately*, far exceeding the `₱4,500` cost of the hardware.
3. You do not have to worry about the customer abandoning your software in Month 2 because they are locked in for the year.
